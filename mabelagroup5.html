EXPERIENCE 1: WHICH OF THE VARIOUS BROADCASTING SOFTWARE SHOULD I USE IN STREAMING?

Describe the broadcasting software your group chose

We chose Melon for its simplistic and easy-to-understand interface. We think, as students, itll be more suited for use since we dont have an expensive setup or expensive equipment to run bigger broadcasting softwares on our laptops.

EXPERIENCE 2: WHAT EQUIPMENT DO I NEED TO LIVE STREAM?

Brainstorming Details:

We started brainstorming on the different equipment well need to use in order to have a successful and smooth broadcast. We thought about the costs of the equipment, like what type of equipment will be pricey and what will be more budget-friendly.

List down the equipment you will use and explain why you chose that equipment.

Laptop or PC/Cellphone(or other mobile devices): Our devices will need to have at least 4-8 GB of RAM to run a good broadcast.Webcam (recommended to be clear and not laggy): For better video quality.Tripod (If youre using a smaller device): For other small devices or if were going to use smaller devices for our broadcast.Mic: For better audio quality.Lights/Good lighting: Along with a good camera, well need good lighting for our viewers to see us.Good internet connection: We need to make sure our broadcast runs smoothly without any lag or delays. Specifically, our connection should reach speeds of 2-15 Mbps (according to Dacast.com). We dont need it to be extremely fast, we just need enough for us to get our broadcast running.Headset

Guidelines to keep in mind

Do's

Dont's

Be as anonymous as you can. Anything you put out on the internet can be used against you.


Disable private messages.


When posting pictures of any documents, make sure you have your information blacked out or erased. Or don't post it at all.


Back up your data.


Do not give away personal/sensitive information about yourself, such as full name, personal social media accounts, address, etc.


Do not use the same password over and over again.


As much as possible do not share images/videos of the town/city you live in. It's like showing your address.


(Related to example above) Dont share too many photos of you being away from your house as your house can be targeted by burglars.
